## Requirements

- The delivery vehicle
- A portable monitor
- A portable power supply (used to power the screen)
- A mouse and keyboard

## Instructions before starting the delivery vehicle 

Before turning on the delivery vehicle, a (portable) display screen needs to be connected to the vehicle via HDMI. Unlock and open the cover at the lower end of the delivery car, with the HDMI port on the left and the USB port next to it, which can be used to insert a mouse and keyboard.
