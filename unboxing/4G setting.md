Insert a data SIM card into the 4G modem.
Ensure the wired connection to the 4G modem is active.
Access the router's configuration page by navigating to 192.168.1.1 (default username/password: admin/admin).
In the modem dashboard, go to the last tab on the left (flash drive icon).
Navigate to the 3G/4G settings tab at the top.
Verify the SIM card settings (e.g., APN, ISP details, and location). Let us know if you need assistance with UK SIM card configurations.
Save the settings and restart the connection via the dashboard.
Validate the connection to ensure proper routing.
